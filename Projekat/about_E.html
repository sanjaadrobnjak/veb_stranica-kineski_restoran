About "Zmaj" Restaurant


Welcome to "Zmaj" Chinese Restaurant, your favorite place for authentic Chinese delicacies in the heart of the city. With a rich selection of dishes prepared from fresh ingredients and traditional recipes, "Zmaj" Restaurant brings you the true taste of China.

Our menu offers diverse options for every guest - from delicious appetizers such as spring rolls and pork dumplings, to aromatic main courses like Kung Pao Chicken, Sweet and Sour Pork, and Mapo Tofu, all the way to irresistible desserts such as mango pudding and mooncakes. Each dish is prepared with care and love to provide you with a unique gastronomic experience.

At "Zmaj" Restaurant, the atmosphere is warm and pleasant, with carefully chosen decor that evokes the spirit of Chinese culture. Our friendly and professional staff is here to assist you in choosing dishes and to provide you with excellent service.

We invite you to visit "Zmaj" Restaurant and enjoy the richness of Chinese flavors. Whether you come for a family lunch, a romantic dinner, or a business meeting, we guarantee you an unforgettable gastronomic experience.

Welcome and enjoy!


Awards and Recognitions

We are proud to announce that "Zmaj" Chinese Restaurant has been recognized for its excellence in the culinary world. Over the years, we have received numerous awards and accolades that highlight our commitment to quality and authentic Chinese cuisine. Some of our notable achievements include:

  • Best Chinese Restaurant Award 2022 - Awarded by the Belgrade Restaurant Association in 2022 for our exceptional food and service.
  • Top Culinary Experience 2023 - Recognized by the International Food and Beverage Association in 2023 for providing a unique and unforgettable dining experience.
  • Chef of the Year 2023 - Our head chef, Petar Petrović, was honored with this award in 2023 for his innovative approach and mastery of traditional Chinese cooking techniques.

These accolades reflect our dedication to providing the highest quality dining experience for our guests. We are grateful for the recognition and strive to continue exceeding expectations with our delicious dishes and impeccable service.
